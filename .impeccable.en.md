# Design Context

[中文版本](./.impeccable.md)

## Users

- The primary users are Chinese mobile users who clean up their own or their family's phone photo libraries.
- Typical usage happens in short sessions: commuting, before sleep, after the library becomes cluttered, or when the app is reopened from a reminder.
- They care about three things more than anything else: seeing clearly, judging correctly, and deleting safely.

## Brand Personality

- Minimal
- Crisp
- Trustworthy
- Localized
- Smooth

The interface should not feel like a generic utility, an admin dashboard, or a cloud AI product. It should feel like a careful, reliable, Chinese-localized on-device photo assistant.

## Aesthetic Direction

- Primary direction: a restrained but warm mobile utility interface.
- Visual tone: light, clean, trustworthy, never flashy, never information-heavy, never marketing-driven.
- Context assumption: users quickly review anomalous, duplicate, or low-quality media on a phone and decide whether to keep or clear it.
- Anti-direction: avoid admin-dashboard styling, neon futurism, heavy glassmorphism, or gradient-heavy AI-template aesthetics.

## Design Principles

1. The media itself stays primary; tags and actions stay secondary.
2. Each moment should communicate one job only: scanning, filtering, reviewing, or deciding.
3. Every high-risk action must feel reversible, understandable, and predictable.
4. Chinese copy must read like a real product, not like a translation or a technical explanation.
5. Motion should reinforce steadiness rather than showmanship.
